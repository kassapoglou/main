Two-Way Data Binding

When dealing with forms, inputs, and sliders, you need the UI to update your Vue data, and your Vue data to update the UI. This continuous sync is called Two-Way Binding, powered by the v-model directive.

1. The v-model Directive

Place v-model on any <input>, <select>, or <textarea>. It automatically binds the element's value to a variable in your data() object.

<!-- As the pilot types, pilotName updates instantly! -->
<input v-model="pilotName" type="text">

<p>Logging in as: {{ pilotName }}</p>

2. Behind the Scenes

v-model is actually just syntax sugar. Under the hood, Vue is combining v-bind:value with @input to keep everything synchronized in real-time without you having to write manual event listeners!
