Structuring Waypoints

When logging crew members, cargo manifests, or jump coordinates, we must group related items together. HTML uses **Lists** for this. There are two main types:

1. The Wrapper: <ul> and <ol>

First, you must define the *type* of list.
• <ul> (Unordered List) creates a bulleted list where order doesn't matter.
• <ol> (Ordered List) creates a numbered sequence (1, 2, 3...).

2. The Items: <li>

Inside your wrapper, every single item must be wrapped in an <li> (List Item) tag. You cannot put raw text directly inside a `ul` or `ol`.

<ul>
  <li>Earth</li>
  <li>Mars</li>
</ul>
> RENDER OUTPUT:
  • Earth
  • Mars
