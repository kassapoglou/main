The Flexbox Matrix

Arranging structural blocks perfectly used to be difficult. Now, we use Flexbox. By applying display: flex; to a parent container, all of its direct children become "flex items" and automatically arrange themselves into a row.

Aligning on the Cross Axis

By default, flex items stretch or stick to the top. The align-items property controls how items align vertically (the cross axis).

flex-start
center
flex-end
.flight-deck {
  display: flex;
  align-items: center;
