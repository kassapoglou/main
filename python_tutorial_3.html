2. Order of Operations

Python follows standard math rules (PEMDAS). Multiplication and division happen before addition and subtraction. You can use parentheses () to force a calculation to happen first.

# Multiplication happens first (2 * 5 = 10, then 10 + 10 = 20)
power = 10 + 2 * 5
print(power)
> OUTPUT: 20

# Parentheses force addition first (10 + 2 = 12, then 12 * 5 = 60)
power = (10 + 2) * 5
print(power)
> OUTPUT: 60
